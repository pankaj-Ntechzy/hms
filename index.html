How to apply

For students who want to apply for BHMS course

What after NEET 2020 dilemma of most students is solved and thanks to the private college seats since the domicile requirements are less stringent and students can apply to these seats in most states of their choice. All candidates throughout the country who qualify NEET 2020 can apply for the seats in Naiminath Homoeopathic Medical College, irrespective of their domicile, caste or state of residence, by filling the application forms of respective state counselling authorities. The same state counselling authorities which conduct the counselling for state quota seats conduct the counselling for private colleges as well. While some states have already begun their application process, most of them will release the NEET 2020 state counselling applications (for private colleges) after NEET result is declared.

Why Choose Naiminath?

India's leading Homoeopathic college with excellence in research, education, and clinical practice.

Indoor Patient Department

Naiminath is the only Homoeopathic Institution globally where regular IPD cases are admitted and treated through Homoeopathy. Documented success stories showcase our clinical excellence.

Evidence-Based Research

Each case is recorded and analyzed using research tools. Naiminath was the first Homoeopathic college permitted by ICMR, CCRH, and AYUSH to conduct COVID-19 research.

NABH Accreditation

We are the first Homoeopathic Institute in India to receive NABH accreditation, ensuring top-tier patient care and hospital standards.

World-Class Faculty

Our faculty includes renowned homoeopathic doctors from across India and abroad, including Dr. Pradeep Gupta. They bring real-world experience into the classroom.

In-House Pharmacy & Lab

Our advanced pharmacy prepares customized medicines. Students gain direct exposure to formulation and clinical use, supporting hands-on learning.

Student Engagement

From the first year, students participate in OPD/IPD activities, gaining valuable clinical exposure alongside theoretical learning.

Super Speciality Hospital

With ICU, NICU, OTs, and modern technology, our hospital provides a real-world training ground for future doctors. NABH accreditation sets it apart from the rest.

Affiliated with AYUSH University

Naiminath is affiliated with Mahayogi Guru Gorakhnath AYUSH University, Gorakhpur, aligning our programs with national AYUSH standards, ensuring quality and recognition across India.
